
A tiny glossary (easy terms)
- AI model vs. AI agent: Model generates outputs; an agent uses those outputs to perform actions and make decisions.
- Tool use: The ability to call external apps and APIs as part of the agent’s workflow.
- Autonomy: Degree to which the agent can act without human input.
- Human-in-the-loop: Humans review or approve certain actions, often used for safety.
- Policies and constraints: Rules that guide what the agent is allowed or not allowed to do.
