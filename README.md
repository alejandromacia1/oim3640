## My Projects: 
- [First Project] (https://github.com/alejandromacia1/oim3640/tree/main/projects/miniproject_1) 

My project is an interactive Python command-line restaurant guide and menu simulation system that is designed to personalize the dining experience of an individual, based on its user preferences. Hence, it intends to simulate a real restaurant ordering process. During the first part of the process, users begin by entering their preferred budget level, cuisine type, and desired restaurant vibe (atmosphere). After that, the system generates a tailored restaurant recommendation based on user inputs and the program presents a customized menu that offers two entree options, two main course options, as well as two dessert options. The prices of the menus are adjusted based on a budget pricing multiplier, simulating the different dining experiences and their corresponding price levels.
